About us

Established in 2015, the Wake Up cafe is an independent counter-service cafe' residing just off the main street of beautiful, Boston city, serving coffees 
 made with locally roasted Buddy Brew beans since 2015, besides it host to music and art shows,& much more.Our approach reflects the people we serve.
    We are passionate yet practical.We understand the value of things. We love our history while keeping our eyes on the future.
                       We are diverse yet the same. The city's energy binds us together. 
      It drives us to be the best.We are committed to quality.From the coffee we source, to the precision with which it is roasted and packaged, 
                                     to our constant training and pursuit of knowledge.
